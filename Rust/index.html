Rust Code Lock Passcode Generator

Helping you create and remember random passcodes since 2017.




This app will generate a code for your code locks in Rust that should not be human guessable. Often times players pick easy to remember codes like 6969, 1337 or 2017 or fallback to their bank PIN which means other players can guess their codes easily or players end up giving up sensitive information to thep layers they share bases with. With the introduction of guest codes, coming up with and remembering unique codes becomes even more important.

This generator will take into account:

  • Ascending/descending numbers (1235, 9876)
  • Repeating/duplicate patterns (1313, 2336)
  • Years (1991, 2016)
  • Four corners (1379, 9137)
  • Edges (4862, 2864)
  • L shaped patterns (7410, 8963)
