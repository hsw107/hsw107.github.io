About

Our Team

At Split Endz Salon & Spa, our amazing team and personalized services will have you walking out of our salon feeling like a "new and better you". We offer a personal, friendly approach to styling, coloring and cutting your hair. Our highly trained staff of stylists and master colorists are at the forefront of today's color and cut techniques. To match their skill, we use only the best hair treatment products. Our stylists use true techniques as the foundation of their craft, while constantly searching for the new and improved processes.

Our stylist Michele Sandon is also a licensed Esthetician that preforms all skin care services in our private spa room and uses the entire line of Dermalogica Skin Care© products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest degree of customer service in a professional and relaxed environment. As industry leaders, we're dedicated to up dating our skills and product knowledge by attending advanced training workshops and seminars. We strive to create the ultimate experience for every client. At Split Endz Salon & Spa, excellent customer service is not just a priority. It is our culture.
